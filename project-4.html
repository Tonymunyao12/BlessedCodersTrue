Project 4

Cleaning Services Project - an android based application that connects any user (person) that is need of cleaning services to the service provider.

Live Link

Project Overview

The project is inspired in many ways by seeing people who do cleaning services for a living. The receiver of the service post that they are need of specific cleaning services and the system matches them with the service provider.

This project is inspired by wanting to creating job opportunities for people within the community.
