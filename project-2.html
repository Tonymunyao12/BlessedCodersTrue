Project Overview

The project entails combination of scripts(shell script) which are used in identifying and taking down spammer's domains(by reporting the flagged domains to the hosting providers).

The spammers are targeting Bitcoin users. They give out a bitcoin address under the impersonation of an influencial account (i.e. crypto influencer Elon Musk ), this address is used as a giveaway/donation.
